\outl0\strokewidth0 \strokec2 Summary: The program will generate top 5 stocks for each market (Japan and Hong Kong in our case) by Short Ratio. Short Ratio is a percentage of outstanding short positions over total outstanding shares for a given stock. It is an indication of how heavily the stock has been shorted (and still not covered or bought back) relative to the outstanding (listed) shares.\
\
We crawled data from Securities and Futures Commission and Tokyo Stock Exchanges website. \
